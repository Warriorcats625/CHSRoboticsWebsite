Centennial High School FTC Robotics Club

Our vision is to create a challenging and supportive atmosphere where each member expands their knowledge of concepts such as engineering, programming, and marketing. Along with creating this kind of atmosphere we aim to help our local community by supporting local businesses, performing outreach, and spreading awareness of FTC and STEM.

Want to join the club?

Our club meets every Tuesday and Thursday in Room 406 (Mr. Watson's Room) from 2:15 - 3:30 pm. Anyone interested in participating in FTC, working with a team, and/or learning the process of making a robot is welcome to come anytime!

Reminder: You don't need any experience to join our club. Everyone starts somewhere. This is a learning experience and we are here to help each other.

If any concerns or questions come up contact us through our Instagram or Email.
